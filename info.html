August 31st

Torino

How to get there:

This is the day where most of you will probably arrive. You can fly directly to Turin or fly to one of the airports in Milan.

If you do the latter, you can either:

  • go to downtown Milan, then take a train to Turin (the high speed train Frecciarossa leaves every 40 min).

  • take a shuttle bus from Malpensa airport to Turin (i.e., this bus from Malpensa-MXP airport for 22 euros).

Where to stay:

We recommend staying in Turin on Saturday. There is a lot to do in the city and we will love for you to join us for dinner in the evening at Il Ciacolon, Giorgia’s mother’s restaurant. We have reserved some rooms at a special price at these two hotels:

  • Hotel Roma is in the heart of Turin, so it is perfect for visiting the city as everything will be walking distance. It is also conveniently located near the train station and the metro. To book a room, just call them and mention that you are going to the “Grisot-de Arcos” wedding. The price they offered us is listed below and includes a continental breakfast:

    • Comfort Single room: 75 €
    • Comfort Double room: 100 €
    • Comfort Triple room: 125 €

  • Hotel Villa Savoia is located in a more residential area near the river Po and the hills that frame the city. It is a little bit further from the center (15 mins by bus, which stops right in front, and 20 mins walking), but it is closer to Giorgia’s house and the restaurant where we will host the dinner. Compared to the other hotel, it also has more stars (4 stars) and a swimming pool. The offer they made us, also including continental breakfast, is the following:

    • Single room: 75 €
    • Double room: 95 €
    • Triple room: 120 €

Alternative hotels:

In case these options don't work for you, below is a list of additional hotels that you can check out.
We didn't make any special arrangements with them but feel free to contact them on your own:

  • La foresteria degli artisti: Adorable Bed & Breakfast owned by friends located in the center of Torino.
  • Cesarino Suite: They have bigger apartments so it might be a good option for families.
  • Cascina San Vito: Elegant hotel in the hills of Torino

September 1st

Castelnuovo

How to get there:

Both the wedding and the reception will be near Castelnuovo Don Bosco, a cute city about 40 minutes by car from Torino. For those of you who are not planning on renting a car, we will offer a shuttle service from Torino to Castelnuovo Don Bosco and, later in the day, to the wedding and back to your hotel in Castelnuovo. If you are interested in the shuttle service, please let us know here.

Where to stay:

Hotel Ciocca: We recommend staying in this charming hotel in the main square of Castelnuovo Don Bosco. The following prices include breakfast:

  • Single room: 45 €
  • Double room: 65 €
  • Triple room: 85 €
  • Quadruple room: 100 €
